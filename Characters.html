Character Page
The World of Falling Kingdoms

Characters

Auranos

Cleiona Aurora Bellos

Cleo is one of the four protagonists of the series. She is a princess of Auranos, the younger daughter of King Corvin Bellos. Cleo has been raised in luxury for the first sixteen years of her life and initially, she is not very strong. However, as the story unfolds, she gains maturity and insight. Her first venture into the unknown to help her sister Emilia ends up changing her and helps shape her into a strong, capable young woman fit to become a queen.


Paelsia

Jonas Agallon

Jonas is the son of a Paelsian wine seller. After his brother is killed, he rises to become a leader of the Paelsian rebellion seeking to destroy Auranos's seeming opulence and lavish wastefulness. Jonas is hot-headed and impetuous, and has a burning desire for vengeance against his brother's murderer. In Rebel Spring, he leads the rebels against cruel King Gaius.


Limeros

Magnus Lukas Damora

Magnus is the firstborn son of King Gaius and crown prince of Limeros. He harbors secret feelings for his younger sister Lucia, who does not return them. Magnus aspires to be exactly like his father, but he lacks the cold and calculating ruthlessness his father possesses. Magnus hides his true feelings behind a mask and struggles with the morality of his and his father's actions.

Lucia Eva Damora

Lucia is the younger child of the Damora family. She later learns that she was in fact adopted because of her prophesied powers as a sorceress. Although Lucia loves Magnus as a brother, she does not return his illicit feelings for her. Her powers awaken when she defends her brother against the witch Sabina, and they enable King Gaius to conquer Auranos. She struggles to control her overwhelming powers and is in desperate need of help and guidance.
